# BitForge Protocol ($FORGE) Tokenomics

BitForge Protocol introduces the universal AI coordination infrastructure on Virtuals Protocol, enabling seamless collaboration between AI agents across all industries and use cases. The $FORGE token powers this ecosystem, facilitating agent coordination, revenue sharing, and governance participation.

**Token Specifications**:
- **Name**: BitForge Protocol
- **Symbol**: $FORGE  
- **Total Supply**: 1,000,000,000 FORGE
- **Blockchain**: Base L2 (Virtuals Protocol)

## Economic Mechanisms

### Deflationary Mechanisms

#### 1. Performance-Based Burns
**Trigger**: Failed strategies or underperformance
**Rate**: 1% of allocated tokens burned when strategies fail to meet benchmarks
**Purpose**: Maintain quality standards and reduce supply

#### 2. Transaction Burns
**Rate**: 0.1% of all trading fees permanently burned
**Volume**: Based on agent coordination frequency
**Impact**: Continuous supply reduction with ecosystem growth

#### 3. Governance Burns
**Trigger**: Malicious or spam proposals
**Amount**: Proposal stake burned if deemed harmful
**Mechanism**: Community voting on proposal validity

### Inflationary Mechanisms

#### 1. Staking Rewards
**Base APY**: 8-12% for standard staking
**Performance Bonus**: Additional 2-5% based on strategy success
**Loyalty Multiplier**: Up to 2x rewards for 12+ month locks
**Source**: Trading fees and protocol revenue

#### 2. Agent Incentives
**Purpose**: Reward high-performing agents
**Rate**: 5% of net profits distributed to top agents
**Criteria**: Success rate, user satisfaction, innovation

#### 3. Community Rewards
**Purpose**: Incentivize ecosystem participation
**Amount**: 2% annual emission for contributors
**Activities**: Content creation, bug reports, strategy testing
